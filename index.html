5 Bean Coffee

We are an independently owned coffee house proudly serving up CrimsonCup Coffee and loose leaf tea. Our cozy little shop located in Reynoldsburg, Ohio offers free Wi-Fi, TV and a convient drive thru. We have something for everyone! Our experienced, helpful staff will serve you up a great cup of coffee in a friendly, cozy, atmosphere.
